Competitive Innovation Mock Trial Champion, Coach, Pioneer

I currently coach 40+ students in Mock Trial, our team won State in both the High School and Jr. High divisions last year. In High School, I had the most decorated career of any student in Utah History placing in the top 10 at the National High School Mock Trial Championship every year except the year it was cancelled due to COVID. That year, my team won 1st and I was the top individual competitor in Online Mock Trial’s first competition to provide a national competition in place of nationals. I was part of a team that founded OMT (though I was sequestered from the case decision for competitive fairness) offering the student perspective and pioneering new ballot techniques. Along with my team, I consulted the National Board and several states in applying our innovations and taking mock trial online during the pandemic. Currently working to bring Mock Trial to BYU.
